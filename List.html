RICE COOKER

An electric pot invented to boil or cook rice with a minimum amount of supervision.

It's simple and convenient to use as it switches from cooking to warming mode when the rice reaches the right temperature. It also comes in a variety of sizes and styles.

MICROWAVE OVEN

An electric oven that employs electromagnetic waves to heat and cooks food by causing food particles to fluctuate.

KITCHEN STOVE

An appliance, often known as stove or a cooker, that is used to cook food and generally contains burners that heat up when turned on.

PRESSURE COOKING

TOASTER

A small electric appliance designed to expose various types of sliced bread to radiant heat, browning the bread so it becomes toast.

AIR FRYER

Most people love fried food though they are conscious of the oil for health reasons. An air fryer is a healthy boon to satisfy your fried food cravings. You can fry anything from chicken to french fries and onion rings to samosas with minimal oil in an air fryer. The food is crispy from the outside with a tender inside without the health risks of excess oil. Easy to store and clean, it makes your cooking easier.

MIXER

Any  baker knows how laborious it is to stir bread dough or cake batter. Mixers make it easy to combine wet and dry ingredients, like flour and eggs. Some mixers come with dough-hook attachments for kneading.

BLENDER

A blender is a kitchen appliance that is used to crush, mix and emulsify food. Generally, a blender consists of a tall glass, food containers with a removable lid made with a filler hole to add the ingredients.

ELECTRIC KETTLE

An electric appliance, that has a self-contained heating unit, for heating water, and automatically switches off when the water reaches a boiling point or at a preset temperature below 100 degree celsius.

FREEZER

A refrigerated compartment, cabinet, or room for preserving food at very low temperatures.

REFRIGERATOR

A home appliance or compartment operated to store foods and beverages that must be kept chilled. 

It comprises thermally insulated compartment and a heat pump that circulates heat to the external setting, resulting in a cold temperature to keep food fresh.
